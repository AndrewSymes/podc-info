Parents With Deaf Children | About

Overview

This website is intended to provide answers to commonly asked questions by parents with a Deaf or Hard of Hearing child.. This project is a culmination of efforts from students at Lamar University

Mission Statement:

Our Mission is to provide helpful and accurate information for parents with Deaf or Hard of Hearing (HoH) children. We students at Lamar University understand that being a parent with a Deaf or HoH child can bring many challenges. The information we provide will allow parents to feel highly confident that they can raise their child to their full potential.

Our goal is to provide you with resources and support through your journey!
